NUS-ISS INTERNSHIP

Role

Research Assistant (UX Research)

Team

Digital Innovation & Design (DID)

Timeline

May 2023 - August 2023
Introduction

Over the summer, I joined Institute of Systems Science at the National University of Singapore (NUS-ISS) as a research assistant.

I worked with the Digital Innovation and Design team on the content creation of Blended Learning Programmes, all in pursuit of improving student engagement and learning experiences.

Project Highlights

UX Research - Gather insights about students' motivations, needs, and pain points through research methods (e.g. interviews, usability testing etc.) My interview transcript was also used as sample material in the UXD blended learning course.

Project Highlights

Executive Education Programmes (EEP) Report Template - Propose a reusable report template that can help DID and other teams monitor and improve course performance. Generated visualizations using real data for initial analysis and refining, followed by further assessments and improvements.

Project Highlights

Explore Service Design Strategies - Participate as teaching assistant in Executive Education Programmes and assist students in service design research. Guide students in generating jobs-to-be-done insights, creating affinity map etc.

Learnings and Takeaways

Starting my first work term abroad at the top university in Asia was an extremely challenging experience, especially when it comes to different communication styles and working culture.

Previously, I mostly worked as a "problem solver" who designs and provides solutions directly to costumers. Here, I am more like a "mediator," who translates customer insights to help stakeholders grasp and align with the design direction. Shifting from a service provider mindset was tough, but it was definitely a meaningful learning experience to approach different kind of design.
